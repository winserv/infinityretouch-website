üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Get started with Infinity Backdrop Cleaner in 5 simple steps:

Install & Activate

Download from Creative Cloud, install, and activate with your license key.

üé® Detailed Workflow

1. Prepare Your Image

For best results, use white, gray, or light-colored seamless backdrops. Ensure the subject is clearly separated from the background with good studio lighting.

2. Mask & Prepare Phase

The plugin uses AI to detect your subject and creates a protective mask. If the detection isn't perfect, use Advanced Controls to adjust Expand and Feather settings.

3. Paint over imperfections

Hold Alt (Option) to sample color from a clean area. Use a large, soft brush and paint generously over shadows, footprints, or wrinkles. Paint freely over the model's edges!

4. Process & Finalize

Click "Process Image". The plugin generates a seamless backdrop, applies 16-bit frequency processing, and adds natural grain to prevent banding.

Can I use it with RAW files?

Yes! We recommend converting your RAW layer to a Smart Object first. This maintains non-destructive editing and maximum bit depth.

Does it work with dark backgrounds?

Optimized for light-colored backdrops. For black or dark-blue floors, use the Eraser tool on the 'Original Background' layer to refine results.
