Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Infinity Retouch ("we"), a product of WINSERV TECNOLOGIA DA INFORMACAO, is committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency about how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection

We collect the absolute minimum data required for the software to function and for us to provide support:

  • Personal Data: Your Name and Email address, provided during checkout.
  • License Data: A Machine Fingerprint (anonymized hardware hash) for license validation and to manage your 2-device limit via our provider, Keygen.sh.
  • Payment Data: Processed securely by our merchant of record, Kiwify. We do not store or have access to your credit card details.

2. Usage of Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To deliver your unique license key via email.
  • To validate your software activation and prevent piracy.
  • To provide you with customer support when requested.

Local Processing Engine

Important: Infinity Retouch is a local plugin. Your photos never leave your computer. We do not upload, store, or have access to any of the images you process, ensuring total security for your clients' work.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data only with essential and secure service providers who are critical to our operations:

  • Keygen.sh (Licensing Server)
  • Kiwify (Payment Processing)
  • Make.com (Automation for email delivery)

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sales@infinityretouch.com.
