Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

At Infinity Retouch, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. However, because our products are digital software delivered via Internet download, we generally offer refunds on a case-by-case basis.

1. 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a full refund within 7 days of your purchase if:

  • The software is technically incompatible with your system (Adobe Photoshop CC 2023+ on Windows or Mac).
  • The software fails to function as described, and our support team cannot resolve the issue.
  • You purchased the product by mistake and have not activated the license key.

2. Non-Refundable Circumstances

We may decline refund requests if:

  • More than 7 days have passed since the purchase.
  • The license key has been activated and used extensively.
  • The refund is requested due to a change of mind after successful use.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us via email at sales@infinityretouch.com.

Please include your Order ID and the email address used during purchase. We will review your request and process approved refunds within 3-5 business days. The funds will be returned to your original payment method.
