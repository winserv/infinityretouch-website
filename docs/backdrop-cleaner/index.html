üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Step 1

Open Your Image

Open a photo with a seamless backdrop in Photoshop. Works best with 16-bit files.

Step 2

Prepare for Painting

Click "Prepare for Painting". The plugin uses AI to detect your subject and create a protection mask.

Step 3

Paint Imperfections

Hold Alt (Option) and click to sample the background color. Paint over wrinkles, dirt, or shadows on the floor.

Step 4

Process

Click "Process Image". The plugin will generate the seamless backdrop while preserving original textures.

üí° Pro Tip: Use the Eraser tool on the "Original Background" layer to recover any subject details that might have been painted over.

üé® Detailed Workflow

1. Subject Masking

The "Prepare for Painting" phase uses AI to separate the subject. If the detection isn't perfect, use "Show Advanced Controls" to adjust Expand (area size) and Feather (edge softness).

2. Sampling & Painting

Always sample the color close to the area you want to clean. For large areas, use a large, soft brush. For floor wrinkles, paint precisely over the folds.

3. Blur & Noise

The final step applies a seamless blur. If the floor looks too "fake", reduce the Blur Radius or increase Noise in advanced controls to match the original grain.

Can I use it on dark backgrounds?

The plugin is optimized for light-to-medium backdrops. For black backgrounds, you may need to adjust the mask manually.

What happens if I change my computer?

Simply deactivate the license on the old machine first. If you don't have access to the old machine, contact support.
