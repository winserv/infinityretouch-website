üöÄ Quick Start Guide

Get started with Infinity Backdrop Cleaner in 5 simple steps:

S1

Install & Activate

Download from Creative Cloud, install, and activate with your license key.

S2

Open Your Image

Open a photo with an infinite backdrop in Photoshop (works best with white/gray backgrounds).

S3

Prepare for Painting

Click 'Prepare for Painting' button. This creates an AI subject mask and configures your brush.

S4

Paint Over Imperfections

Hold Alt (Option) and click to sample color. Then paint over shadows, wrinkles, or footprints.

S5

Process Image

Click 'Process Image' to generate the seamless 16-bit backdrop automatically.

üí° Pro Tip:

Don't worry about painting over your subject! The plugin preserves the original in a separate layer, so you can easily recover any details using the eraser tool on the "Original Background" layer.

üé® Detailed Workflow

1. Open & Assess

Load your photo. Best results with white, gray, or light colored infinite backdrops. The plugin handles shadows, footprints, and wrinkles.

2. Subject Mask

Click "Prepare for Painting". The plugin creates an AI-powered edge refinement around your subject, protecting them from backdrop changes.

3. Paint Cleanup

Hold Alt (Option) to sample color from a clean area. Use a large, soft brush and paint generously over shadows, footprints, or wrinkles. Paint freely over the model's edges!

4. Process & Finalize

Click "Process Image". The plugin generates a seamless backdrop, applies 16-bit frequency processing, and adds natural grain to prevent banding.

Can I use it with RAW files?

Yes! We recommend converting your RAW layer to a Smart Object first. This maintains non-destructive editing and maximum bit depth.

Does it work with dark backgrounds?

Optimized for light-colored backdrops. For black or dark-blue floors, use the Eraser tool on the 'Original Background' layer to refine results.
