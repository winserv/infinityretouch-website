Step 1

Open Your Image

Open a photo in Photoshop. Works best with studio photos (seamless paper, cyclorama) where the subject is clearly visible.

Step 2

Prepare for Painting

Click the blue button "Prepare for Painting". The AI will mask your subject automatically and select the Brush tool.

Step 3

Sample & Paint

Hold Alt (Mac: Option) and click on a clean area of the background to sample the color. Then, paint over the dirt, wrinkles, or shadows you want to remove.

Step 4

Process Image

Click "Process Image". The plugin will generate a clean background while preserving the original shadows in a separate layer.

‚ùì FAQ & Troubleshooting

How to activate?

After purchase, copy the key from your email. Open the plugin > Activate > Paste the key.

Processing is slow?

High-resolution images (50MP+) take more time due to the 16-bit processing engine. This ensures quality.

"Subject not found" error?

Ensure the layer is visible. If the AI fails, make a rough selection of the subject before clicking "Prepare".
